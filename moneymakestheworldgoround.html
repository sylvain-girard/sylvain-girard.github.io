Money Makes the World Go Round

JAGDA JISPA

Selected work of the Japan Graphic Designers Association International Student Poster Awards 2020, and exhibited at the Tokyo National Art Centre in December of 2020.

This poster was created for JAGDA’s ISPA awards in 2020, with the theme of ‘Money’. It’s essentially communicating the title of ‘Money Makes the World Go Round’ literally, depicting an Earth that requires a constant flow of money to turn, though with the side effect of destroying the planet itself. It was completing modeled with Cinema 4D and rendered with the Arnold renderer, with some post processing in Photoshop.
