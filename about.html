Hey I'm
Sylvain

(silvan)

A Melbourne-based designer and 3D generalist, graduate of a diploma of visual merchandising at RMIT in 2017 and a bachelor of communication design at RMIT in 2021. My practice spans across most fields, though with a focus on integrating technology with my design, through unconventional methods and mediums, be it animation, coding, AR/VR, or 3D. My 3D practice involves 3D art, modelling, rendering, AR/VR integration, 3D printing and production, and other bits and pieces. I'm always trying to expand my skillset to create new and unique ideas.
