These are a set of posters based on a fictional festival, the Future Food Festival. It is a festival focused on the foods of the future and how society will have to adapt to a world with a shortage of food that is grown/made using traditional methods.Â The medium is a digital poster, to be used as online advertisments and the like, therefore I took advantage of being able to incorporate some animation to help communicate that 'futuristic' feel, though in a fun way. The two posters share the imagery of a conveyor belt churning out some 'future foods', though one is more conventional than the other with static typography for greater legibility.
